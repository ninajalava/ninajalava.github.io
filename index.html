Hi and welcome!

 

I'm Nina, a textile designer and natural dyer based in Stockholm, Sweden. I craft by hand in small batches using plant dyes on natural fibers. I undertake customized dyeing projects, and I currently make and sell soothing eye pillows, peace silk scarves and multi-purpose tenugui.

 

I look for near and faraway places to display my work and offer collaborations and wholesale prices to shops. Contact me for more information or just to say hi. P.S. There are some colorful images of my work and play loading below, it just takes a little while...
