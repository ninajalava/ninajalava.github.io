Hi and welcome! I am Nina and this is where I showcase my work and play.

 

I like simplicty and beautiful things. I like things that last. I have no formal degree in crafts and design but learn mostly through experimenting. Nature is my largest source of inspiration. Let's try to protect it!

 

Crafts I do and things I ♡:

Textiles / Ceramics / Jewelry / Photography / Travel / Climbing / Sunshine

 

 

 

News 

April 2015 – My eye pillow is featured in the new number of Elle Decoration Sweden. The spread highlights an increasing prevalence of eco, fairtrade and local production in the world of interior design and handicrafts.

 

Nov 2014 / Now – My eye pillow is now for sale at manos, a calm oasis with selected crafts and design products. I help out in the shop on Saturdays. Come by and say hi! The shop is located on Renstiernas gata 22 in the SOFO area of Södermalm, Stockholm. www.manos.se
