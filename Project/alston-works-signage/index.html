Alston Works Signage

Signage designed for the Alston Works community in High Barnet.


The set of buildings has one postcode for 3 different entrances and it was a necessity to create a signage to help direct deliveries and visitors to the right companies and flats.


Alston works is an old victorian fake teeth factory. Inspiration for the sign and typography comes from the original factories signage from the late 20th century, with a modern input. The bright orange colour has been chosen as a reference as the landmark Alston Works orange 'gate'.


Signs are printed on reused materials related to the warehouse environment such as Pallets and mdf.
